The Awards

All-Time Awards

League history · 3 seasons · The records that define the dynasty

🏆
Most Championships
coalj
2 titles — 2023 and 2024. The only back-to-back champion in league history. His own dominance triggered the Dynasty Reset Rule that stripped his roster. The most decorated manager in the league and the cautionary tale about what happens when you win too much.
👑
All-Time Wins Leader
coalj — 61-23
Barely edges arwandl3r (60-24) for the all-time wins crown. One win separates the dethroned king from the new champion. The margin is thin and it's closing — arwandl3r gained ground in 2025 while coalj slipped to 13-15. This battle is far from over.
📈
Mr. Consistency
rbenson4 & GMadison
20-8, 19-9, 20-8. Never below .500. Never below 19 wins. In three years, this team has not once had a losing record. That kind of sustained performance in a competitive dynasty league is genuinely rare — and it still hasn't won them a championship. The most frustrating award in the trophy case.
💀
Best Record Without a Ring
rbenson4 & GMadison — 59-25, 0 titles
59 wins. 0 championships. The highest PF in the league in 2025 (3,122.26). The numbers scream dynasty contender — the results say something different. This is the award nobody wants to win, and yet here we are. The best team to never win it all in Dysfunctional Dynasty history.
🔥
Biggest Single-Season Turnaround
plund3342 — +10 wins
From 5-23 in 2024 to 15-13 in 2025. A 10-win improvement in a single offseason, powered by an aggressive reset of the entire roster. The biggest turnaround in league history. LaPorta Potty went from the punchline to the comeback story everyone is watching.
📉
Biggest Fall from Grace
coalj — 24-4 champion to 13-15
From the peak of two consecutive 24-4 seasons to a 13-15 record post-reset. An 11-win drop from his dynasty peak. The sharpest decline in league history — made uniquely painful because it was self-inflicted. coalj's own back-to-back triggered the rule that ended his era.
🎢
Most Volatile
DaWerni98
9-19, 4-24, 9-19. Three seasons of inconsistency so consistent it's almost impressive. The reset year was supposed to change the trajectory — and technically it did (4-24 to 9-19 is improvement) — but returning to the exact starting point after three years tells its own story. Consistently inconsistent.
🔒
The Iron Throne
coalj — Back-to-Back Champion
The only manager in league history to win back-to-back championships. The only manager to trigger the Dynasty Reset Rule. The only manager to have their dynasty both built and destroyed by their own success. Love him or hate him, coalj wrote the first chapter of this league — and the rest of the history is written in reaction to what he built.
