The Dynasty Reset Rule

Win the championship two consecutive years and you trigger an optional redraft for the entire league. Every team chooses: keep your roster or start from scratch in a 10-round startup draft. The catch? The back-to-back champion's redraft is mandatory. The crown comes at a cost.

The Aftermath

The reset shook the entire league. coalj — the two-time defending champion — was stripped of his dynasty roster and forced to rebuild from scratch. He went from 24-4 to 13-15. The crown cost him everything.

plund3342 made the most of it — jumping from 5-23 to 15-13, a 10-win improvement. Gibbs at 1.01 was the foundation.

Meanwhile, the teams that kept their rosters held strong. arwandl3r rode roster continuity all the way to the championship at 22-6. rbenson4 & GMadison led the league in scoring at 3,122 PF.

The lesson? In dynasty, you can reset the roster — but you can't reset the relationships between the players. Chemistry takes time.
